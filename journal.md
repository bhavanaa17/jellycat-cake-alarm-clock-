Starting with the CAD first:

I actually started with CAD first because that is just what I'm more familiar with. I've worked with fusion a lot in my school and I'm also really scared about the electronics so I'm trying to avoid it right now.. I made the base of the cake, which is just one layer of cake, frosting, the legs, and the iconic Jellycat face. Mainly just through a lot of sketches and extrusions - it took me ahwile to create a offset plane for the face and legs as sketching on a round surface was definitely throwing me off. 
I made some space for the PCB and wires, but I haven't made the proper place for it yet. Will do that soon! I also made a little place for the display to go, though I am a little confused about how that would work when I design my PCB because I need to have strawberries on the top but also need the display to be on the front side. I might switch it up and make the display on the top of the cake with the strawberries and candle. Maybe this means I can make the face a little bigger :) I also made little holes so that I can connect the top part after I CAD that too.
